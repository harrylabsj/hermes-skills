# Platform Fit

Prefer JD for:

- branded electronics
- appliances
- standardized goods
- urgent delivery expectations
- cases where after-sales certainty matters
- purchases where 自营 or official flagship trust is worth paying for

Be more cautious when:

- the best visible price is from a marketplace seller
- promotion rules depend on account-only coupons
- model names or bundle contents are unclear
- review history is thin, repetitive, or heavily promotional
- the user only wants the absolute lowest price
- final price depends on checkout-only address, invoice, payment, or coupon rules

Switch or compare when:

- `pdd-shopping`: user prioritizes the lowest practical price or subsidy mechanics
- `taobao-competitor-analyzer`: user wants same-item comparison against Taobao
- `tianmao`: user prioritizes flagship-brand trust over JD self-operated logistics
- `find-items`: user has not chosen a platform yet
