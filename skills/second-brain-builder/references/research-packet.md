# Research Packet

Use this template when expanding a knowledge point into a second-brain concept page.

## Concept Packet Shape

```markdown
# <知识点>

## AI 注：概念卡片（<YYYY-MM-DD>）

### 一句话定义
<用 1-2 句定义概念，包含它处理的问题、核心机制、适用边界。>

### 概念边界
- 是：<它是什么>
- 不是：<容易混淆但不是它的东西>
- 关键判据：<如何判断一个例子是否属于这个概念>

### 来龙去脉
- 背景问题：<为什么需要这个概念>
- 前置脉络：<之前有哪些相关概念/理论>
- 提出者与时间：<谁、何时、在哪篇论文/哪本书/哪个场景中提出或经典化>
- 后续发展：<后来如何被修正、扩展、应用或争议>

### 结构与机制
- 核心构件：<组成部分>
- 运作方式：<因果链条或推理结构>
- 适用条件：<何时有用>
- 失效条件：<何时会误导>

### 相关知识点
- 上位概念：[[...]]
- 相邻概念：[[...]]
- 对立/区分概念：[[...]]
- 应用场景：[[...]]

### 推荐阅读
1. <Author>. <Title>. <Year>. <为什么读它>
2. <Author>. <Title>. <Year>. <为什么读它>

### 与我的第二大脑连接
- 已有关联笔记：[[...]]
- 可补的链接：[[...]]
- 后续可扩展：[[...]]

### 来源与可信度
- <source title/link>：<支持了什么>
- 可信度：高/中/低
- 待核实：<不确定点>
```

## Source Quality Rules

- Origin claims need primary or near-primary evidence whenever possible: original paper/book, author interview, publisher page, university profile, DOI/arXiv/ACM/IEEE/JSTOR/Project MUSE, or reputable archive.
- Definitions may use surveys, textbooks, encyclopedias, and canonical secondary sources, but should not rely only on blog posts.
- Reading recommendations should be real and useful, not decorative. Prefer fewer sources with reasons.
- For books, include author, title, year, and why it belongs in the path.
- For papers, include author(s), title, year, and DOI/arXiv/conference/journal when discoverable.
- When a source is not accessible or the claim is inferred from secondary material, say so.

## Reading Path Pattern

Order readings by learning utility:

1. Best modern overview or textbook chapter.
2. Canonical paper/book where the concept was introduced or stabilized.
3. One contrasting or critical work.
4. One applied work connected to the user's interests.
5. Optional advanced survey or frontier paper.
